Our Story

As a founder of iGrab Cafe Mr. Fahad Al Shehhi hadambition to deliver the most unique type of high-quality food and services for the guests across variousfamous sources to make sure that they are the mostunique and successful Emirati productions…

So, after that the idea came up why not to have it on one spot and support them and at the same time easy marketing for all of them and you as a costumer can have anything you want from at least 30 family business at one hub ( one stop shop )

Our story began in 2016 when there was high demandon home based business, and there was lack of servingon time and place, in addition to that they neededsome support for marketing and developing theirpresentation and delivery.

Our Vision

The basic idea and vison of our operation is not just serving ordinary coffee like others, No, Under the one roof, iGrab Cafe is aiming to serve the highest possible quality of Coffee Beans ( Speciality Coffee ) and Sweets and Desserts for our customers. having the best quality food is something everyone’s aiming to satisfy their passion. We also have a vision which focuses on the theme art of ‘Giving’ that is the expansion process of homemade business gets strategic position to present and offer their products.

Igrab Meaning

Families in UAE are apprised to connect with the neighbours,‘iGrab’ is the word express a positive feeling and share theirwillingness to have a supper together. The idea of iGrab Cafe isgenuinely inspired by those kindnesses and generosity practicedin Emirati culture.

(UAE Local Meaning name if iGrab), People who really followthe principal of giving and wish to serve others purely from theirheart where there is no motive to gain any benefit in return byinviting them to the house and hospitalize them. The Culture inUAE is originally practiced in the art of giving, where giving itback is considered as a blessing.
